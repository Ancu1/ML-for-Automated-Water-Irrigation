Key Findings

  • Available Water Capacity has a strong correlation with soil texture components, particularly clay content.
  • Soil organic matter (represented by TOC) shows significant influence on water-holding properties.
  • The dataset contains a good distribution of soil textures, making it suitable for ML model training.
  • Feature selection has identified the most relevant parameters for irrigation prediction.

Recommendations for ML Model Development

  • Consider ensemble methods like Random Forest or Gradient Boosting for irrigation predictions.
  • Focus on Available Water Capacity as a key target variable for irrigation scheduling.
  • Incorporate weather data in future models to enhance prediction accuracy.
